Citing PHYLUCE¶

If you use PHYLUCE for your research, please cite the following manuscript

[Fair:2012a]Faircloth BC, McCormack JE, Crawford NG, Harvey MG, Brumfield RT, Glenn TC (2012). Ultraconserved Elements Anchor Thousands of Genetic Markers Spanning Multiple Evolutionary Timescales. Systematic Biology 61: 717–726. pmid: 22232343 doi: 10.1093/sysbio/SYS004.
